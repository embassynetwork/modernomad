The Embassy San Francisco

The Embassy San Francisco is our experiment in creating a home built around purpose and intention. We value curiosity, analysis, questioning, and engagement. This is not a house for by-standers! If you like to jump in and be part of the fray, we think you'll like it here.

Density Ideas +
Collaboration

A community of creatives and professionals sharing space in informal ways. Opportunities for organic and emergent discussions and collaborations. We're suckers for serendipity.

Action

We build community through doing stuff together! We share food, space and ideas. Think cooking and coworking but also reading clubs, art projects, skill shares, or just watching a movie. We open our doors frequently; we often try new approaches to organizing ourselves.

Focus

We often work long hours. Our work may spill into home, but usually on purpose and with joy. We strive to do what we love. Daytimes at the house are more downbeat, and evenings often involve impromptu meals and wide ranging discussions.

Part of Something Bigger

The Embassy Network is a new generation of residential spaces around the globe, rooted in intention, collaboration, and a modern nomadic disposition. In the Embassy Network, members find a global family of travelers and seekers, and a place to call home as we travel for work and collaborations.

Participate

We host a salon series on most Thursdays, and a community dinner every Sunday. We've got a Maker Monday series that's currently happening once a month, and various one-off events. Hear about upcoming events on our mailing list!

Stay with Us

Guests are a cornerstone of our house. Part of our experiment here is seeking out those who are traveling the world for curiosity, passion, and collaboration, and welcoming them into our home. Would you like to become a member of our global family?
